📖 How to Use Zyps Task Pro

1️⃣ Add your first contact
Tap the ＋ Add Task button on the Today tab → fill in the contact's name, company, and what you need to do next → tap Save.
2️⃣ Track follow-ups
Set a follow-up date on each contact. They appear on your Today tab when due. Tap ✅ to mark done, 📞 to log a call, or ✉️ to log an email.
3️⃣ Use the Kanban board
Go to the Pipeline tab to drag contacts between stages: New Lead → Contacted → Proposal → Closed. Great for tracking deals.
4️⃣ Draft messages with AI
Go to the AI tab → select a contact → choose Email or WhatsApp → tap Draft. The AI writes a professional message using your contact's details.
5️⃣ Voice commands
Enable the voice assistant below. Say your wake word (default: "Hey Pro") then a command:
"Show today" · "Find Rahul" · "Open pipeline" · "Remind me to call Amit tomorrow"
6️⃣ Backup your data
Go to Backup & Sync below → sign in with Google for automatic sync, or tap 📥 Backup my data to save a file to your device.
💡 Tip: Install the app on your phone — tap 📲 Install App above for one-tap access from your home screen. Works offline, no internet needed.
